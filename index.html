Basis of Inception …

Surya Engineering Group (SEG) is teamed up in 2015 to provide simple and optimum solutions for solar module mounting structures.

SEG believes that simplifying the Module mounting aspect enables more installation of power plants and that supports the ambitious Solar Power targets of our nation.

SEG strives to offer most optimally designed products & solutions which are a very good import substitution available at right price, quality & time.

SEG Standard Solar PV MMS products

1a : Sheet roof, Long Rail system (Modules in Landscape)

Features:

  • MMS anchored to the roof purlin using suitable Self Dril Screws.
  • Module bottom clearance options of 50mm, 78mm & 110mm till sheet crown flat.
  • EPDM gasket to ensure no leakage
  • MOC of structure: Aluminium alloy, as per IS-733-Grade-63400-WP (6063T6)
  • MOC of all assembly fasteners: Stainless Steel, Grade-SS-304.

1b : Sheet roof, Short Rail system (Modules in Portrait)

Features:

  • MMS anchored to the roof sheet over two crown flats.
  • Module bottom clearance options is of 50mm & 78mm till sheet crown flat.
  • EPDM gasket to ensure no leakage.
  • MOC of structure: Aluminium alloy, as per IS-733-Grade-63400-WP (6063T6)
  • MOC of all assembly fasteners: Stainless Steel, Grade-SS-304.

1C : Tiled Roof top MMS

Features:

  • MMS anchored to the Rafter of truss OR to RCC roof below the Tiles.
  • Module bottom clearance is about 100mm.
  • Modules can be mounted either in Landscape or Portrait orientations.
  • MOC of structure: Mounting brackets of MS+Galvanised and rails are of Aluminium alloy, as per IS-733-Grade-63400-WP (6063T6)
  • MOC of all assembly fasteners: Stainless Steel, Grade-SS-304.
